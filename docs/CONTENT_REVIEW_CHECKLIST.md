# Content Review Checklist

Use this checklist before adding a story to the app or publishing it to
Firestore.

## Copyright Safety

* The story is original or adapted from public-domain/traditional source material.
* No modern copyrighted adaptation text was copied.
* No branded characters, settings, or unique modern plot additions were copied.
* Source notes are recorded.

## Child Age Suitability

* The story fits ages 3-6.
* Sentences are short enough for read-aloud use.
* The conflict is understandable and not overwhelming.
* The ending feels safe.

## Amharic Readability

* Amharic text sounds natural when read aloud.
* The story avoids overly literal translation.
* Each page has 1-3 short sentences.
* Repeated phrases are helpful and not excessive.

## Moral Clarity

* The theme is clear.
* The moral is gentle, not shaming.
* The child can understand the lesson through the story action.

## Cultural Sensitivity

* The story avoids outdated stereotypes.
* Characters are treated with dignity.
* Family and community roles are represented respectfully.
* Any cultural references are reviewed for care and accuracy.

## Illustration Safety

* Prompts avoid frightening imagery.
* Prompts avoid graphic danger, injury, or punishment.
* Character expressions are warm and child-friendly.
* Images do not include text that should be rendered by Flutter instead.

## Audio Readiness

* Story text has a good read-aloud rhythm.
* Page breaks support natural narration pauses.
* Character voices, if any, are simple and respectful.
* Audio metadata can remain null until recording is ready.

## Final Checks

* No scary prompts.
* No copied copyrighted adaptations.
* Local JSON validates.
* Local images exist.
* Story can be loaded through the current fallback chain.
