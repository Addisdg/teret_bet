# Design Inspiration

Teret Bet can learn from successful children's reading and audio apps without
copying their visual identity, characters, layouts, or proprietary experiences.

## Inspiration Categories

### Epic-Style Library Browsing

Useful lessons:

* Covers should be large and easy to scan.
* Story categories can help families find the right mood.
* Progress indicators help children return to stories.

Teret Bet direction:

* Keep the library calmer and less commercial.
* Prioritize Amharic titles and warm illustrations.

### Khan Academy Kids-Style Friendly UI

Useful lessons:

* Large tap targets help young children.
* Friendly icons reduce reading burden.
* Soft colors feel welcoming.

Teret Bet direction:

* Use simple controls and minimal clutter.
* Keep parents comfortable reading aloud from the same screen.

### Moshi-Style Calm Audio Experience

Useful lessons:

* Audio experiences should feel peaceful.
* Playback screens should avoid visual noise.
* Bedtime use benefits from gentle color and motion.

Teret Bet direction:

* Future narration should support calm listening.
* Avoid overstimulating animations during reading.

### Audible And Storytel-Style Playback Patterns

Useful lessons:

* Play/pause, progress, duration, and resume behavior are familiar.
* Download state should be clear.

Teret Bet direction:

* Add audio controls only when useful.
* Keep playback simple enough for parents and children.

## Teret Bet Visual Direction

The visual direction should be:

* Warm storybook
* Soft colors
* Large readable Amharic text
* Calm interface
* Simple icons
* Minimal clutter
* Illustration-first
* Friendly but not noisy

Screens should feel like a quiet children's book shelf, not a marketing page.
